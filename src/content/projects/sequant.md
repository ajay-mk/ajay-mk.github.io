**SeQuant** is a framework for symbolic and numerical tensor algebra. It is designed
to automate the derivation and efficient implementation of many-body electronic
structure methods, including higher-order coupled cluster theories.

## What it does

- Manipulates second-quantized expressions symbolically.
- Lowers symbolic tensor expressions to optimized numerical code.
